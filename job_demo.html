      About ESG.AI

      ESG.AI is an innovator at the intersection of two of the hottest areas - Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) and artificial intelligence (AI) - on a mission to transform how organisations explore, define, and share their ESG goals. We enable companies to visualise, model, and optimise their own approach to sustainability and are passionate about helping our customers to envision and implement meaningful change.


      About the Job

      As a senior full-stack developer, you will play a critical role in taking our SaaS products to the next stage in their evolution — we have exciting growth plans that require a diverse and nimble skill set! You will work in a cross-functional team composed of developers, data scientists, engineers, academic researchers, and designers to transform ideas from the team into our suite of ESG strategy planning and optimisation products.


      We’re interested if you have:

      • 6+ years of development experience
      • Experience with some or all of our tech stack: React, Kotlin or Java, Spring Boot, PostgreSQL, Kubernetes, AWS, Azure
      • Bachelor’s degree in computer science or equivalent
      • An obsession with getting the details right


      We’re extra-interested if you have

      • Experience with Firebase, ElasticSearch
      • Jira or Trello background
      • Exposure to AI and ML
      • Team leadership experience


      We believe that a company is a reflection of its employees.
